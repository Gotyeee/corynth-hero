Built for researchers who verify.

Corynth Labs exists to raise the standard for research peptide supply. Every batch ships with batch-matched documentation, traceable to an independently verified COA.

Research peptides deserve real documentation.

Corynth Labs supplies research peptides tested by an independent third-party lab and shipped with batch-matched documentation. The commitments below describe how we run. The COAs page shows what that looks like in practice.

Four commitments. Every order.

01

Third-Party Verified

Independent third-party verification on a sampling program — HPLC purity and FTIR identity confirmation, with batch-matched COAs published per shipment.

02

Batch Traceability

Every vial carries a batch number that matches the COA on our site. What we test is what we ship.

03

Transparent Documentation

COAs are visible to anyone before purchase — no login, no request forms. Verification should be the default, not a favor.

04

Verify Without Asking Us

Every COA we publish is independently verifiable through the testing lab's public record system. No need to contact us, request documents, or trust our word. Look up the task number and unique key from the COA direct on the lab's site.
