Questions, answered.

Common questions about products, testing, shipping, and research use. For anything not covered here, reach out directly.

SHIPPING

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 5–7 business days in standard insulated packaging. Delivery time depends on your location and carrier. Tracking info is sent by email when your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes — US orders over $200 ship free. Orders under $200 include a flat shipping fee calculated at checkout.

Do you ship outside the United States?

No. Corynth Labs ships within the United States only. Orders to addresses outside the US cannot be processed at this time.

PAYMENT

What payment methods do you accept?

Cryptocurrency is the primary payment rail; debit and credit cards are accepted as a secondary option. Crypto orders qualify for bulk discount tiers of up to 25% on larger research-facility volume.

Why is crypto preferred?

Crypto payments settle faster, carry near-zero processing fees, and cannot be reversed through chargebacks. Those savings are passed back through the bulk discount tiers, which apply to crypto orders only.

Are my payment details secure?

Card payments are processed through an encrypted third-party gateway — we never store or see your card details. Crypto payments go directly to our wallet on-chain, with no third party holding funds.

TESTING & COAS

Who tests your reference compounds?

Verification is performed by an accredited third-party analytical laboratory, with HPLC purity and FTIR identity confirmation. Every batch we sell is independently verified before release. Batch-matched COAs are emailed to the customer after every purchase, and available on request before purchase.

How do I verify a vial matches the COA?

Every vial is labeled with a batch number. After your order, the COA PDF for that batch is emailed to you — confirm the batch number on the emailed COA matches the batch number on the vial label. If the two do not match, contact us immediately.

STORAGE

How should lyophilized vials be stored?

Lyophilized vials should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct light. Long-term storage at freezer temperature extends shelf life. Storage requirements change in solution — handling protocols are the responsibility of the purchasing facility.

What is the shelf life of a lyophilized vial?

Properly stored lyophilized reference compounds typically maintain stability for 24+ months. Stability windows in solution are materially shorter and depend on the specific compound. Refer to published preclinical research for compound-specific stability data.

RESEARCH USE

Are these products for human use?

No. All products sold by Corynth Labs are for laboratory research use only. They are not intended for human consumption, veterinary use, diagnostic procedures, or any clinical application. By ordering, you confirm you are a qualified researcher using these products in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Do you provide handling or research-protocol guidance?

No. Corynth Labs does not provide handling instructions for human use, application guidance, or any non-research protocol. Research protocols are the responsibility of the qualified researcher and the purchasing facility, in accordance with their study design and institutional policies.
