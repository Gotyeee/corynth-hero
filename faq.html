Questions, answered.

Common questions about products, testing, shipping, and research use. For anything not covered here, reach out directly.

SHIPPING

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 5–7 business days in standard insulated packaging. Delivery time depends on your location and carrier. Tracking info is sent by email when your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes — domestic US orders over $200 ship free. Orders under $200 include a flat shipping fee calculated at checkout. International shipping is available at checkout, with rates varying by destination.

Do you ship internationally?

International shipping is available for most destinations at checkout. Customs duties, import taxes, and regulatory restrictions are the responsibility of the buyer. Some countries restrict the import of research peptides — please verify local regulations before ordering.

PAYMENT

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cryptocurrency (primary) and debit/credit cards (secondary). Paying with crypto unlocks bulk discount tiers of up to 25% off larger orders.

Why is crypto preferred?

Crypto payments are faster to settle, have near-zero processing fees, and cannot be reversed through chargebacks. Those savings are passed back to customers through the bulk discount tiers, which apply to crypto orders only.

Are my payment details secure?

Card payments are processed through an encrypted third-party gateway — we never store or see your card details. Crypto payments go directly to our wallet on-chain, with no third party holding funds.

TESTING & COAS

Who tests your peptides?

Every batch is independently tested by Janoshik Analytical. Each lot receives HPLC purity verification and mass spectrometry identity confirmation. Full COAs are available publicly on our COAs page and on each product page.

How do I verify my vial matches the COA?

Every physical vial is labeled with a batch number. That batch number matches the COA listed for that product on our site. If you ever receive a vial whose batch number doesn't match what's displayed, contact us immediately.

Why don't you list separate HPLC and mass spec reports?

A single Janoshik COA includes both HPLC purity and mass spec identity confirmation. Listing them separately would just duplicate the same document — one COA per batch is cleaner and more honest.

STORAGE

How should lyophilized peptides be stored?

Store lyophilized (powder form) vials in a cool, dry place away from direct light. Long-term storage at freezer temperature extends shelf life. Once reconstituted, storage requirements change — consult current research protocols for the specific compound being studied.

How long do peptides last?

Lyophilized peptides stored properly typically maintain stability for 24+ months. Reconstituted peptides have a much shorter stability window. Always refer to published research for the specific compound you're working with.

RESEARCH USE

Are these products for human use?

No. All products sold by Corynth Labs are for laboratory research use only. They are not intended for human consumption, veterinary use, diagnostic procedures, or any clinical application. By ordering, you confirm you are a qualified researcher using these products in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Do you provide dosing or reconstitution protocols?

No. We do not provide dosing recommendations, reconstitution instructions for human use, or any guidance on non-research applications. Research protocols should be determined by the qualified researcher using the product according to their study design.
