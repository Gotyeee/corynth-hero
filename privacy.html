Privacy Policy

How Corynth Labs LLC collects, uses, and protects information when you use corynthlabs.com.

01

What we collect.

When you place an order, we collect the name, email address, shipping address, and order details required to fulfill your purchase. Crypto payments are recorded as on-chain transaction hashes; card payments are processed by a third-party gateway and we do not store card numbers. When you visit the site, our hosting provider may log standard request data (IP address, user agent, referrer, timestamps) for security and reliability purposes.

02

How we use it.

We use the information you provide to process orders, ship products, send order-related communications, respond to support requests, and operate the site. We may use aggregated, non-identifying analytics to understand how the site is used and improve it. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising or behavioral profiling.

03

Cookies.

The site uses essential cookies and local storage to keep your cart state between pages and remember basic preferences. We do not use third-party advertising, retargeting, or cross-site tracking cookies. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies; doing so may break cart functionality but will not prevent you from browsing the site.

04

Sharing.

We share information only with the service providers required to operate the business: payment processors, shipping carriers, email providers, and our hosting provider. Each receives only the data needed to perform its function. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to any third party. We may disclose information when required by valid legal process or when necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.

05

Data retention.

We retain order records for as long as needed to fulfill orders, handle returns, and meet tax and accounting obligations under U.S. and Oregon law (typically seven years). Support correspondence is retained for up to two years. You can request earlier deletion subject to those legal retention requirements.

06

Your rights.

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you the right to know what personal information we hold about you, to request a copy, to request deletion subject to legal retention requirements, and to be free from discrimination for exercising those rights. Residents of other jurisdictions with similar laws (including the EU/UK GDPR and other U.S. state privacy laws) have equivalent rights. To exercise any right, email us using the address below; we will verify your identity before responding.

07

Security.

The site is served over HTTPS with TLS encryption. Order records are stored on secured infrastructure with access limited to the people who need it to fulfill orders and provide support. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure; we work to use industry-standard practices and to disclose any incident that materially affects you, in accordance with applicable law.

08

Children.

The site is intended for adult researchers and qualified laboratory personnel. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided information to us, contact us and we will delete it.

09

Changes and contact.

We may update this policy as the business evolves. Privacy questions and data requests can be sent to privacy@corynthlabs.com (or support@corynthlabs.com until that address is live). Mailing address available on request.
