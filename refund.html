Refund & Return Policy

All Corynth Labs products are analytical-grade biochemical reference compounds for laboratory research use only. Because of the chain-of-custody required for reference materials, all sales are final once an order has shipped. The Quality Guarantee below is how we make legitimate issues right.

01

All sales final once shipped.

Once a batch has been pulled, packed, and handed to the carrier, the order is final. We do not accept returns for buyer's remorse, change of mind, or research outcomes. Opened vials cannot be returned under any circumstance — once a seal is broken we have no way to verify the contents on return.

02

Pre-shipment cancellation.

If your order has not yet entered fulfillment, we will issue a full refund on request. Once a tracking number has been generated, the order falls under the Quality Guarantee below.

03

Quality guarantee.

Contact us within 48 hours of confirmed delivery with your order number and photographs if any of the following apply:

• Damaged on arrival. Visibly broken, leaking, cracked, or compromised vials.

• Incorrect product. A vial of a different reference compound or a different specification than what was ordered.

• Failed third-party verification. A vial whose batch number does not match the Certificate of Analysis on file, or independent re-testing that materially contradicts the reported purity or identity specification.

04

Cryptocurrency orders.

For orders paid in cryptocurrency, refunds are issued in store credit or as a reshipment of the affected product. Direct cryptocurrency refunds are not issued due to exchange-rate volatility between purchase and resolution.

05

Card orders.

For orders paid by card, approved refunds are returned to the original card and typically appear in 5–10 business days depending on your bank. Original shipping fees are non-refundable except where Corynth Labs is at fault.

06

Chargebacks.

Disputes should be raised with our support team first. Initiating a payment chargeback before contacting us will result in permanent suspension of the associated account and refusal of future orders. Chargeback fees imposed on Corynth Labs by the payment processor will be deducted from any partial refund issued.

07

Storage and handling.

Each vial ships with the storage conditions printed on the label. Lyophilized reference compounds are sensitive to heat, humidity, and light, so handling and storage after delivery sit with the recipient. The Quality Guarantee covers the condition of the material as we shipped it, not degradation that occurs once it is in your lab.

08

Not covered.

This policy does not cover any claim related to the end-use of a research compound, including but not limited to study results, animal-model observations, biological responses, allergic responses, or any condition asserted by the recipient or any third party. Products are sold as analytical reference materials only.

09

How to start a claim.

Email support@corynthlabs.com (or info@corynthlabs.com until that address is live) with your order number, a brief description, and photographs or test reports as applicable. We respond within one business day.
